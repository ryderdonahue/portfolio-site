Role: Team Member
In 2013, my team and I participated in Micosoft's Imagine Cup competition. Our project won us the United States Imagine Cup, beating over 2,000 other teams and allowing us to compete in the World Finals.
Our submission, help he help, is a suite of applications to help those in need during an emergency or natural disaster, while also assisting emergency response personnel in relief and recovery efforts.
After the competition, we saught to make help me help real. While we were successful in gaining meetings with state and federal government officials, we eventually ran out of funding and each member of the team pursued new opportunities.
Fortunately it appears that our project has inspired a new featureset in FEMA's official disaster response application, so even if help me help isn't the name of the product, it's concept will be carried on to help people in need in the future.
